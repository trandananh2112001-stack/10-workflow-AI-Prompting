# Recruitment Summary: Data Analyst

| Section | Summary |
| --- | --- |
| Job Title | Data Analyst |
| Main Purpose | Collect, analyze, and interpret data to support business decision-making, generate insights, and deliver reports and dashboards that improve performance and efficiency. |
| Five Key Responsibilities | 1. Collect, clean, and validate data from multiple sources.<br>2. Analyze data to identify trends, issues, and business opportunities.<br>3. Prepare regular reports and dashboards for management and business teams.<br>4. Respond to ad-hoc analysis requests from Sales, Marketing, Finance, and Operations.<br>5. Translate findings into clear insights and recommendations while working with stakeholders to inform decisions. |
| Five Essential Selection Criteria | 1. Bachelor’s degree in Data Analytics, Business, Statistics, Economics, IT, or related field.<br>2. 1–3 years of experience in a Data Analyst or similar role.<br>3. Strong skills in Excel and SQL.<br>4. Experience with Power BI, Tableau, or similar dashboard tools.<br>5. Ability to present findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders and manage multiple tasks with attention to detail. |
| Three Preferred Criteria | 1. Experience with Python or R.<br>2. Experience working with business or operational data.<br>3. Understanding of KPI reporting and dashboard development. |
