| Job Title | Data Analyst |
| Main Purpose | Collect, analyze and interpret data to support business decision-making and provide reports, dashboards and insights that improve performance and efficiency. |
| Five Key Responsibilities | 1. Collect, clean and validate data from different sources.<br>2. Analyze data to identify trends, issues and business opportunities.<br>3. Prepare regular reports and dashboards for management and business teams.<br>4. Support ad-hoc analysis requests from Sales, Marketing, Finance or Operations.<br>5. Translate data findings into clear insights and recommendations. |
| Five Essential Selection Criteria | 1. Bachelor’s degree in Data Analytics, Business, Statistics, Economics, IT or related field.<br>2. 1–3 years of experience in a Data Analyst or similar role.<br>3. Strong skills in Excel and SQL.<br>4. Experience with Power BI, Tableau or similar dashboard tools.<br>5. Ability to present findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders. |
| Three Preferred Criteria | 1. Experience with Python or R.<br>2. Experience working with business or operational data.<br>3. Understanding of KPI reporting and dashboard development. |
